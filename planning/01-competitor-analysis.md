## Market Gaps (Opportunities for DividendMapper)

### Gap 1: No true auto-sync winner
Sharesight claims 200+ integrations but requires manual CSV upload in practice. Simply Safe Dividends is entirely manual. Stock Events' broker sync is limited and unclear. **The first product to deliver genuinely frictionless broker sync at an affordable price wins this category.**

### Gap 2: No dual UK/US market owner
- Sharesight dominates UK/international with strong tax reporting
- Simply Safe Dividends owns US conservative retirement investors
- No single product serves both markets with equal depth

DividendMapper can own the intersection: UK investors with US holdings (SCHD in an ISA is an enormous and growing segment), and US investors who want a clean, modern tracker.

### Gap 3: ISA/SIPP + 401k/IRA in one tool
Only Sharesight approaches UK tax wrapper awareness. No US tool handles ISA dividend exclusion. No UK tool properly handles US REIT distributions vs qualified dividends. This is a genuine technical moat — getting this right requires careful implementation and is hard to copy quickly.

### Gap 4: Trading 212 SIPP native integration
T212 SIPP launched February 2026. No competitor has built SIPP-specific dividend tracking for T212 users. T212 has 4.5M accounts and gives users almost no analytics natively. **First-mover window is open right now and will close within months.**

### Gap 5: Affordability with real automation
The market splits into:
- Free tools with no automation (TrackYourDividends, basic tier of everything)
- $200–400/yr tools with limited automation (Sharesight, Simply Safe, Seeking Alpha)

There is no well-executed product in the £10–15/mo range with genuine broker auto-sync. Stock Events at $50/yr is close but lacks tax features and web depth. **DividendMapper Pro at £15/mo is underpriced relative to value if broker sync works reliably.**

## Positioning Statement

> DividendMapper is the only UK/US dividend tracker that auto-syncs directly with your broker (Trading 212, Schwab, Fidelity, Interactive Brokers), understands ISA, SIPP, 401k and IRA tax rules, and shows your projected income — all in one place, starting free.

**Primary differentiators (in priority order):**
1. Trading 212 SIPP native sync — first mover, unique to UK market
2. Genuine broker auto-sync without manual entry
3. UK ISA/SIPP + US 401k/IRA tax awareness in one product
4. Free tools that build SEO traffic and trust before asking for payment
5. Price: Pro at £15/mo vs competitors at £21/mo–$39/mo
