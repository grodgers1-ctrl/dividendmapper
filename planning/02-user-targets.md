## Assumptions

| Assumption | Value | Basis |
|------------|-------|-------|
| Free-to-Pro conversion | 3–6% of MAU | B2C fintech average is 3–6%; targeting upper end because dividend investors actively manage real money |
| Pro-to-Premium conversion | 0.5–1.5% of MAU (target 1% at scale) | Premium features (AI analyst, tax reports) have specific trigger points (tax season, portfolio size) |
| Monthly churn — blended | 6% until data proves otherwise | Industry average for monthly-billed B2C fintech |
| Annual plan churn | 3% | Annual commitment reduces churn significantly |
| SEO materialises meaningfully | Month 6+ | New domain sandbox typically delays meaningful organic traffic 4–6 months |
| T212 launch spike | Month 4 | Single biggest UK acquisition event — post on 4.5M-user T212 forum day of launch |
| US SnapTrade launch spike | Month 7 | US communities (r/dividends 802K, r/personalfinance 19M) unlocked by broker sync |
| Addressable market (realistic active users) | 1.8M–4.5M globally | 2–5% of ~90M API-reachable UK/US accounts who would actively use a dividend tracker |

## What Each Growth Spike Requires

### Month 4 — Trading 212 spike (+850 MAU)
The biggest single UK acquisition event. Requires:
- T212 API integration fully built, tested on 50 beta users
- T212 forum post timed to coincide with launch
- r/UKInvesting announcement post
- SIPP-specific features live (annual allowance tracking, projected SIPP retirement income)
- T212 CSV fallback already built (in case API behaviour changes)

Expected: 800–1,000 signups in the first week from T212 forum alone. T212 has 4.5M users and gives them almost no dividend analytics natively — this fills a real gap they're actively asking for.

### Month 7 — US SnapTrade spike (+1,100 MAU)
The biggest single US acquisition event. Requires:
- SnapTrade integration for Schwab, Fidelity, Robinhood, Vanguard US
- r/dividends launch post (802K subscribers)
- Product Hunt re-launch with US-facing messaging
- Reddit Ads test ($200–400/mo) targeting US dividend subreddits
- First US YouTube creator partnerships in motion (Tier 2, 50K–300K subs with affiliate deal)

### Month 11 — Premium spike (AI analyst + tax season)
UK self-assessment deadline is January 31; US tax deadline is April 15. Month 11 targets January for the UK window. Requires:
- AI analyst beta behind a waitlist — gated access creates urgency
- Tax report feature generating HMRC-compatible dividend income summaries
- In-app upgrade prompts: "Generate your 2025/26 dividend tax report — upgrade to Premium"
- Email campaign to all Pro users: "Tax season is here. Your report is one click away."

## Revenue Sensitivity

Small changes in conversion rate have large effects on MRR. At Month 12 MAU of 7,500:

| Free-to-Pro rate | Pro users | Est. MRR (Pro only) |
|-----------------|-----------|---------------------|
| 3% | 225 | ~£3,375 |
| 5% (base case) | 375 | ~£5,625 |
| 8% | 600 | ~£9,000 |
| 17% (Month 12 target with Premium) | 1,275 | ~£17,400 (Pro) + £27,000 (Premium) |

**The Month 12 target requires both Pro and Premium to perform.** If Premium lags (tax reports or AI analyst underperform), Pro alone at 17% conversion still reaches ~£17,000 MRR — below target but a sustainable business.
